# The Art of War

## Overall Picture

*The Art of War* is a canonical military strategy text that combines principles of judgment, timing, deception, and situation awareness.

## Distinctive Features

- It is short but densely packed.
- It is principle-driven rather than purely procedural.
- It becomes clearer when read with context and decomposition.

## Internal Logic

The text argues that good strategy depends on understanding conditions before acting, shaping the situation, and winning with the least waste possible.

## Top 10 Knowledge Points

1. It is a canonical-text topic.
2. Context matters before quotation.
3. Many lines are principle statements, not rigid commands.
4. Deception is one tool, not the whole system.
5. Preparation matters before direct action.
6. Terrain and conditions change decisions.
7. Leadership quality affects outcomes.
8. Timing matters as much as strength.
9. Reading only summaries misses the texture of the original text.
10. Overview + original text + decomposition is the safer study path.
