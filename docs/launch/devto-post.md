# Dev.to Launch Draft

## Title

I Built a Top-Down Learning Skill for Agents That Explains Hard Topics and Finds the Right Materials

## Hook

Most learning prompts explain, but they do not teach. They also make the learner find the material first.

## Problem

Hard topics often fail in two ways:

1. The explanation starts too deep and loses the learner.
2. The material set is either weak, random, or disconnected from the explanation.

## Solution

TopDown Tutor fixes that by combining:

- a fixed teaching rhythm
- source acquisition
- canonical-text decomposition
- understanding checks

## Example

You can ask it to teach an API, React Hooks, probability, or a canonical text like *The Art of War*.

## CTA

Try the public skill package, preview the README, and adapt the workflow for your own learning agent stack.
